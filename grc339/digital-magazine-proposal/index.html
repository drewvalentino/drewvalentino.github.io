Trinkets and Trash

Unbound Inc

"Trinkets and Trash" focuses on the joy of collecting trinkets, and turning "trash" from our world into new creative, transformed projects. A world of cuteness and junk combined is the perfect recipe for collectors and journalers alike. Trinkets and Trash speaks to the maximalists, the artists, and the collectors our there in the world looking for a bit of fun! Hop right in.

Unbound works to push the boundaries.
